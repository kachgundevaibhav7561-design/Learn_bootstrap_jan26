Pune, the cultural capital of Maharashtra, India, is a dynamic city known for its rich history as the seat of the Peshwas, its modern status as a major IT and education hub (earning it the nickname "Oxford of the East"), and its vibrant festivals, particularly the Ganesh Utsav. Located on the Deccan Plateau, it offers a blend of historical sites like Shaniwar Wada and Aga Khan Palace, natural beauty with surrounding hills, and a thriving industrial and academic scene.
