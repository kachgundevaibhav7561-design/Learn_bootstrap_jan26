Gazania

gazaniaGazania is a bright and attractive flowering plant known for its vivid colors and sun-loving nature. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to South Africa. Gazania flowers are famous for their daisy-like shape and colorful petals, usually found in shades of yellow, orange, red, pink, and white, often with dark patterns at the center. Gazania blooms open fully in bright sunlight and close during cloudy weather or at night. This flower symbolizes joy, strength, and positivity. It is commonly grown in gardens, borders, pots, and landscapes because it is low maintenance and drought-tolerant.
